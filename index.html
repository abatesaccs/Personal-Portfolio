About Me

Hi! My name is Andrew, and I've been interested in programming for as long ad I can remember. Growing up my family had a Windows 98 desktop that I would tinker with whenever I could. I have always been fascinated with how things work, and seeing what I can make from the pieces im given. I'm currently interested in 6502 assembly, because I love video games, and would like to know just how hard it was to make them when they were in their infancy.

Background

I started my experience with programming by learning Java. I used an IDE to write and compile my programs throughout highschool, and in my senior year migrated to C# and Unity. Before making the switch to C#, using a hardware simulator, I made a 64k computer with logic gates. While making the computer, I learned basic assembly, and low-level processing.
